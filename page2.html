About app

For what? To have an efficient tool that:

  • will help to make self-improvement as a daily practice
  • allows to work on requests that you don’t cover with a psychotherapist
  • will give an opportunity to self-improvement for those who cannot visit a psychologist due to lack of money or time
  • or because of fear / embarrassment, or due to the absence of a good psychologist
