Feel yourself visiting psychotherapist:

  • Advanced voice interface to dive deeper into the process
  • All needed education is included

This mobile application is like having a personal psychologist at your fingertips, but in the end, it's just you and your mobile phone. With various tools and features, the app helps guide you towards greater self-awareness and personal growth, much like working with a therapist. Whether it's managing anxiety, conquering fears, or improving relationships, the app offers a safe and confidential space for exploring your inner world and achieving your goals.

For what? To have an efficient tool that:

  • will help to make self-improvement as a daily practice
  • allows to work on requests that you don’t cover with a psychotherapist
  • will give an opportunity to self-improvement for those who cannot visit a psychologist due to lack of money or time
  • or because of fear / embarrassment, or due to the absence of a good psychologist

App contains

Diary

  • Keep a diary of your behavior and emotions that you’d like to change or improve.
  • The ability to track changes and progress over time.

By tracking your patterns and progress over time, you can identify areas where you excel and areas where you can improve. This helps you stay accountable and motivated to achieve your goals. With the help of technology, it's now easier than ever to keep a diary and track your progress right from your mobile device.

Diagnosis

  • A step-by-step self-psychotherapeutic diagnosis process to understand the root core of undesirable behaviors and emotions.
  • Information on how to use the information gained during the diagnosis process to determine the most appropriate psychotherapeutic transformation process.

A comprehensive self-psychotherapeutic tool that guides you through a step-by-step diagnosis process to uncover the root cause of negative behaviors and emotions. With this valuable insight, you can determine the most effective path for personal transformation and growth. Additionally, the app provides guidance on how to use the information gathered during the diagnosis process to support the transformation journey and achieve positive change.

Transformation

  • A step-by-step guide to the psychotherapeutic transformation process, tailored to the user's specific problem and skills.
  • Using the most powerful and safe psychotherapeutic methods that are possible to use for self-transformation.

Our app offers a comprehensive and personalized psychotherapeutic transformation process that is tailored to the user's specific needs and skills. Our step-by-step guide allows users to effectively navigate through the process, while utilizing the most powerful and safe psychotherapeutic methods for self-transformation. With our app, users can achieve lasting change and growth towards a happier and healthier life.
